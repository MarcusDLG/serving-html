Pasta Challenge Winner

JimBob Cooter has been a long time fan of food challenges and has not shied away from this one. If he's not directing Matty Stafford to whip a ball downfield, he's at a restaurant trying to expand his stomach. That means that this man was ready to take on the 4 pound challenge!

The four pound challenge consists of consuming 4 pounds of elk meat spaghetti made with our fresh pasta and our owner harvested elk meat. You have 2 hours to finish your plates or your meal isn't free, so work fast but smart! In order to take over the spot on the website, then you must finish all four pounds in under 1hr and 36 minutes. All times are rounded to the next nearest minute, and tenths and hundredths of seconds will be used only for record-breaking measurement.
